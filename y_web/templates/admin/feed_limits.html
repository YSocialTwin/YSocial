Quick Reference Guide

Feed Limits
These settings control how much external content is ingested during a forum simulation. Higher values increase variety but also startup and refresh costs.

Recommended Use
Keep defaults for small and medium experiments. Increase limits only when you need broader topical variety or longer-running content pools.

Rate Limiting
The Reddit delay should stay conservative enough to avoid throttling when multiple feeds are refreshed in sequence.
