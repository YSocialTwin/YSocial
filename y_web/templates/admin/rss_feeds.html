Quick Reference Guide

RSS Feeds
Use RSS feeds for sources that publish structured article streams. The feed metadata is stored in rss_feeds.json inside the experiment folder.

Direct URLs
Use direct article URLs when you want to pin a specific reading list or when a source does not provide a reliable RSS endpoint.
